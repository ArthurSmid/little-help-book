G-Street Oasis
1175 G St. Springfield
541-214-0260
ccslc.org/g-street-oasis
Provides day shelter and other services for homeless families with children. Laundry, hygiene supplies, bathrooms, kitchen facilities and showers available. Also provides limited transitional housing and referrals for families. To obtain services one must be registered in the program, please call Gail at the phone above to register, or email gstreet@ccslc.org
